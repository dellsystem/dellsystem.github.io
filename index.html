Hi! I'm Wendy, and I live in San Francisco.

My debut book, Abolish Silicon Valley: How to Liberate Technology From Capitalism, a memoir/manifesto about the tech industry, was published in 2020 by Repeater Books.

I've also written a variety of shorter essays about technology and politics, among other things. You can find more of my writing here. I am currently working on a novel.

You can get in touch with me via email (ilostwaldo@gmail.com), Twitter (@dellsystem), or Instagram (also @dellsystem). For press inquiries relating to the book, please contact enquiries@repeaterbooks.com.

I'm being selective about speaking/writing opportunities at the moment. If we don't know each other, and it's not paid, I probably won't do it. Thanks for understanding.
