Hi! I'm Wendy, and I live in San Francisco.

My debut book, Abolish Silicon Valley: How to Liberate Technology From Capitalism, a memoir/manifesto about the tech industry, was published in 2020 by Repeater Books.

I am currently working on a novel, as well as an ongoing column about tech billboards in San Francisco for Bay Area Current.

I've also written a variety of shorter essays about technology and politics, among other things. You can find more of my writing here.

Contact me via email (ilostwaldo@gmail.com), Twitter (@dellsystem), or Instagram (also @dellsystem). I use Instagram to post monthly round-ups of the books I've read.
